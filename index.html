Yet another Hackernews client for iOS

Simple, fast, intuitive and elegant, HNBuddy will make your browsing experience precisely as good as its developper's programming skills permit it to be!

Offline bookmarks

Save the posts you want to read later, even for offline reading.

Also, keep track of all the posts you read and manage your favorite posts as you would on the website.

Expandable, rich discussion threads

Browse all the discussions in an efficient and pleasant way. Enjoy of the well formated, rich content of the comments.

Customisable reader

Activate the reader to take control over the font-size, font-style and theme of each article.

Features

Light & Dark themes

The app comes with 2 themes from which you can choose or let the app choose for you according to the time or to the brightness of your screen.

Free

HNBuddy is entirely free of cost and ads.

Secure

Your credentials are exclusively used to log you in to Hackernews and are not shared with any third-party. The whole "sign in" process is open source and available here.

Fast

The application is optimized to load the content as fast as possible and to render it smoothly.

Simple & Intuitive

The UI integrates all the features using familiar designs that will make you love using the app.
